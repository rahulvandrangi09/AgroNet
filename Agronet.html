Agronet is an intelligent portal designed to help farmers diagnose crop diseases quickly and accurately, providing essential information to protect their yield.

Common Diseases

Late Blight

A fungal disease common in potatoes and tomatoes, causing brown lesions and plant decay.

Powdery Mildew

White powdery growth on leaves, often affecting cucumbers, grapes, and cereals.

Leaf Spot

Causes dark spots on leaves; common in peanuts, beans, and other leafy crops.

Rust

Reddish-brown pustules appear on leaves and stems; frequently affects wheat and beans.

Root Rot

Caused by overwatering or poor drainage, it affects root health, leading to stunted plant growth.

Downy Mildew

Affects leafy vegetables, producing yellow spots and fuzzy growth on the underside of leaves.

Recommended Medications

Just some of the few Medications

Fungicure-X

Used for: Treating fungal infections like powdery mildew and blight.

Applicable crops: Tomatoes, Grapes, Wheat

Dosage: Mix 5ml per liter of water. Spray weekly during early infection signs.

Neem Guard

Used for: Natural pesticide to prevent bacterial and fungal diseases.

Applicable crops: Vegetables, Rice, Pulses

Dosage: 10ml/liter. Spray every 10 days during the season.
